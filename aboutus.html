About Us

Discover the story behind our eco-friendly mobile bus hotel revolutionizing sustainable travel in Botswana and Southern Africa.

Our Story

Solar Stays on Wheels is a pioneering project in sustainable tourism, founded to promote eco-friendly travel across Botswana and Southern Africa. We believe in harnessing renewable energy to create unforgettable experiences without harming the planet.

Our journey began with a vision to combine mobility, luxury, and sustainability. By converting a bus into a solar-powered hotel, we offer travelers the freedom to explore stunning landscapes while minimizing their environmental footprint.
