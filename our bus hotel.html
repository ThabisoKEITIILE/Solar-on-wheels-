Our Solar-Powered Bus Hotel

Experience luxury on the move with our innovative, eco-friendly mobile accommodation designed for sustainable adventures across Botswana and Southern Africa.

Key Features

Solar-Powered

Fully equipped with solar panels for zero-emission energy, powering everything from lights to air conditioning while on the road.

Mobile Luxury

Converted from a premium bus into a 5-star hotel on wheels, offering comfort and style wherever your journey takes you.

Comfortable Accommodations

Spacious private cabins, communal lounges, and outdoor decks for relaxing amid stunning natural landscapes.

Eco-Friendly Design

Built with sustainable materials, water recycling systems, and minimal waste practices to preserve the environment.

Amenities & Facilities

Indulge in a range of premium amenities tailored for eco-luxury travelers. From gourmet meals prepared with local ingredients to relaxing spa experiences, our bus hotel ensures every moment is memorable.

  • Private en-suite cabins with queen beds
  • Fully equipped kitchen and dining area
  • Mobile hot tub and spa under the stars
  • Live entertainment deck with sound system
  • Wi-Fi powered by solar energy
  • All meals inclusive, focusing on sustainable sourcing

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Green Travel Redefined

Our bus hotel runs entirely on renewable solar energy, reducing carbon emissions by up to 100% compared to traditional accommodations. We partner with local communities to support conservation efforts and promote responsible tourism in Botswana's pristine wilderness.

Every aspect—from energy use to waste management—is designed to leave a positive impact on the environment and the regions we visit.
